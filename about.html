About Me

By day, I am an Engagement Manager & Account Lead at a full-service digital marketing & advertising agency. By night, I am a web developer specializing in front-end and back-end technologies.

I specialize in managing web and app development projects that require business acumen & technical expertise.

I've worked with clients such as Coca-Cola, Krispy Kreme, Shaw Floors, Oppenheimer & Co. Inc., Cracker Barrel, and Georgia-Pacific.

While I'm not in tech, I also act as a production assistant in film & other creative projects. I have worked on music videos, video shoots, and commercial photoshoots.

And when I finally have time for fun, I enjoy aerial arts on lyra / aerial hoop as my main apparatus. However, I also enjoy sling / aerial hammock, trapeze, and silk fabrics. I also enjoy roller skating and various flow arts, including fire fans, leviwand, and poi.

I love technical as well as creative pursuits. Ultimately, I want to create art and make the world a better place. Let's build something together.
