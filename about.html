About Me

By day, I am an Engagement Manager & Account Lead at a full-service digital marketing & advertising agency. By night, I am a web developer specializing in front-end and back-end technologies.

As an Enagement Manager, I specialize in managing web development and app development projects that require business acumen & technical expertise.

I've worked with clients such as Coca-Cola, Krispy Kreme, Shaw Floors, Oppenheimer & Co. Inc., Cracker Barrel, and Georgia-Pacific. My background includes account management, IT project management, and business analysis in mobile app development, web development, marketing, advertising, and digital analytics.

In my past life, I have also worked as an IT Consultant, Project Manager, and Business Analyst at Dell EMC (formerly EMC). And I've worked with clients across various industries, including consumer retail, financial services, and healthcare.

When I finally have time for fun, I enjoy aerial arts (lyra/aerial hoop, sling/aerial hammock, trapeze, and silk fabrics). I also enjoy roller skating and various flow arts, including fire fans, leviwand, and poi.

Ultimately, I want to create compelling art and make the world a better place. Let's build something together.
