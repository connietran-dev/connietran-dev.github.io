About Connie

Hi there! I'm Connie Tran. I'm so glad you're here.

By day, I am an Account Lead & Technical Project Manager at a digital marketing & advertising agency.

By night, I am a full-stack web developer specializing in frontend and backend technologies, including Javascript, React, Node, socket.io, Express, MongoDB, & MySQL.

I have over 6 years of experience in the software development industry managing web development and mobile app projects that require business acumen & technical expertise.

During this time, I've delivered a $20 million portfolio of web & mobile projects for clients such as Coca-Cola, Krispy Kreme, Cracker Barrel, Georgia-Pacific, TIAA, and Oppenheimer & Co. Inc.

I received a certificate from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Full-Stack Web Development where I held an A+ average and was our class's selected graduation speaker. I've also volunteered as a speaker and student presentation judge with the TechBridge Technology Career Program which prepares unemployed and underemployed adults for a career in technology.

I've developed a variety of different projects as seen on my Portfolio and GitHub. And I've entered several hackathons as well as won the Wallifornia 2020 MusicTech Hackathon. My professional resume can be viewed here.

On occasion, I moonlight as a production assistant in film & other creative projects and have worked on music videos, video shoots, and commercial photoshoots.

I enjoy practicing aerial arts, including lyra, sling, trapeze, and silk fabrics. I also roller skate on occasion with the Atlanta Roller Derby and enjoy practicing various flow arts, including fire fans, leviwand, and poi. I am also an all-around nerd :)

I love technical as well as creative pursuits. I want to create art and make the world a better place.

I'd love to build something - together.
